About

Where technology meets creativity

The Wilding Foundation is a federally registered charity based on Salt Spring Island, BC. We believe that the intersection of technology and art creates powerful opportunities for learning and growth.

Through grants, programs, and community partnerships, we invest in people and projects that make creative technology accessible to everyone—especially youth.

Projects

What we support

We partner with local organizations to bring technology, art, and education together on Salt Spring Island and beyond.

Fab Lab

A community space for learning and creating with digital fabrication tools like 3D printers and laser cutters. We also make robotics and STEAM courses free for all kids ages 6 to 18 through our Child & Youth STEAM programs.

$200k annually

Catalyst Grants

Providing $3k–$6k grants to artists who need help finishing their visual art. Too often a project remains incomplete because the artist lacks the resources to see it through.

$100k annually

Salt Spring Arts Council

Promoting arts and culture on Salt Spring Island, supporting local artists and cultural programming.

GISS Robotics Club

Encouraging youth to engage in robotics and technology through hands-on learning and competition.

Graffiti Theatre

Supporting local theatre and performing arts, bringing creative expression to the community.
