FabLab

A creative haven at the heart of the community

The FabLab at the Salt Spring Public Library is a community makerspace equipped with 3D printers, laser cutters, design software, and more. It offers islanders of all ages a place to learn, experiment, and create -- all free of charge.

In 2025, the FabLab logged over 1,100 appointments and 3,600 drop-in visits, with STEAM program attendance over 1,000 participants.

“The Fab Lab is a very special place for my neurodivergent child and for our community in general.”

GISS Robotics

Building robots, building futures

The Cyber/Scorpions team brings together students from grades 8 to 12 who design, build, and compete through VEX Robotics. The team has grown to 30 students and seven independently designed robots in a single season.

Helping artists finish what they started
